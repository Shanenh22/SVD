Our story

Serving North Dallas families since 1994.

Spring Valley Dental Associates was founded with a simple mission: exceptional dental care in a comfortable, welcoming environment — without the judgment, pressure, or hidden costs that make so many people avoid the dentist in the first place.

What started as a single-doctor practice has grown into a comprehensive dental team serving thousands of Dallas families. Our philosophy has never changed: conservative, preventive care that helps patients keep their natural teeth for life, delivered with the personal attention every patient deserves.

Today, patients drive from Addison, Farmers Branch, Carrollton, Irving, and across the Tollway corridor to see us — and many have been with us since we opened our doors.

Dr. Nicole Houshmand, DDS

Dr. Houshmand grew up watching dentistry practiced at its best — she's a second-generation dentist who knows firsthand that the chair can feel scary. Certified in conscious sedation, she makes it her mission that anxious patients finally get the care they've been putting off.

"My goal is to make every patient feel heard and comfortable. When patients feel relaxed and informed, they make better decisions about their health — and they actually come back."
