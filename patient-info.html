Your first visit, step by step.

We know that coming to a new dentist — especially after a long time away — takes courage. Here's exactly what to expect so nothing catches you off guard.

1
We listen first
Tell us your concerns, history, and what matters most to you
2
We examine
Comprehensive oral exam, digital X-rays, cleaning
3
We explain
We review everything we see — costs, options, priorities
Important: Nothing gets done without your understanding and consent. We explain every finding and every cost before we proceed. If you want to think about it, take your time — we're not going anywhere.

What to bring

Valid photo ID
Driver's license, passport, or state ID
Insurance card
If you have dental insurance — we'll verify your benefits
Medication list
Any current prescriptions, vitamins, or supplements
Previous X-rays

No judgment — ever

It's been a while? We've heard it before.

The most common thing new patients say when they call us is some version of "I'm embarrassed — I haven't been in years." We hear this every single week. Our response is always the same: we're glad you're here. We don't count the years. We don't lecture. We help.

If dental anxiety is part of what's kept you away, Dr. Houshmand is certified in conscious sedation dentistry. We can make your appointment genuinely comfortable — not just bearable, but comfortable.

Most new patient appointments last 60–90 minutes. This includes a comprehensive oral exam, digital X-rays, a full dental cleaning, and time to discuss your dental history and any concerns. We never rush.
